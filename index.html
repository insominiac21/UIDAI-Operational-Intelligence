Intelligence Framework

This platform transforms raw administrative data into **Operational Intelligence**. By segments districts into six distinct archetypes, we provide authorities with a clear roadmap for equipment refreshes, staff deployment, and local outreach strategies.

OPI Scoring Logic

The **Operational Priority Index (OPI)** is a composite score weighted by three critical drivers:

  • Coverage Gap (45%): Unmet demand and regional saturation.
  • Youth Percentage (30%): Future growth potential and school-age demand.
  • Update Load (25%): Pressure on existing enrollment centers.

Similarity Engine

Instead of simple geographical proximity, our engine computes **Feature-Space Similarity**. By finding neighbors with identical operational profiles, we allow administrators to transplant successful strategies from similar districts across state lines.
