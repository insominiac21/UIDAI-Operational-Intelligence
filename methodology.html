Methodology & Model Logic

Technical audit of the data pipeline and decision heuristics.

Operational Priority Index (OPI)

The OPI is a weighted composite score (0-100) used to rank districts for intervention. It accounts for three primary dimensions:

  • Infrastructural Stress (35%): Log-scaled biometric failure rates and update bottlenecks.
  • Demand Velocity (35%): Percent of school-age children (youth_pct) and enrollment trends.
  • Coverage Deficiency (30%): Current enrollment gap relative to projected demographics.

Intelligence Archetypes (Clustering)

We use K-Means++ Clustering to segment districts into 6 distinct profiles. The model is trained on:

  • Log-scaled Update Load
  • Log-scaled Biometric Stress
  • Youth Concentration (0-14 segment)
  • Absolute Coverage Gap

*Dimension reduction via UMAP $(x, y)$ is used for spatial visualization.

Decision Heuristics (DSS Implementation)

This platform uses Tiered Logic to translate raw priority scores into tactical actions:

OPI > 75: Critical Deployment
OPI > 50: High-Priority Review
OPI > 25: Baseline Optimization
